Empowering Future Leaders Since 1998

Calvin College stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Burgersfort, Limpopo. As an independent private school, we have been dedicated to nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders for over two decades.

Our History

Established in 1998, Calvin College was founded on the unwavering principles of academic excellence, holistic learner development, and meaningful community engagement. For over two decades, we have been shaping the educational landscape of Burgersfort and the broader Limpopo region, providing quality independent education that prepares learners for success in an increasingly complex world.

Our journey began with a vision to create an educational institution that would not only impart knowledge but also instill values, build character, and nurture the unique potential within each learner. Today, we proudly serve learners from Grade R through Grade 12, offering a comprehensive educational experience that spans the formative early years through to matriculation.

Located in Section 8, Farm Leeuw Vallei, Burgersfort, our school provides a safe, structured, and inspiring environment where learners are encouraged to explore their capabilities, pursue their passions, and achieve their dreams. Our facilities, combined with our dedicated staff and supportive community, create the ideal setting for academic and personal growth.

Our Guiding Principles

At the heart of Calvin College lies our school motto: Naviter, Simpliciter, Parce. These three powerful words represent more than just a phrase - they embody the philosophy and values that guide every aspect of our educational approach. They remind us daily of the balance we seek to maintain in developing well-rounded, capable, and principled young people who are prepared to make their mark on the world.

Our Mission Statement

To maximize the abilities of learners within a structured framework of cooperation.

To also help learners develop confidence in their abilities to succeed, emerging resourceful and able to manage their own lives in a world of wide employment.

Our Core Values

Academic Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in teaching and learning, ensuring every learner reaches their full potential.

Character Development

We nurture integrity, respect, and responsibility in all our learners, preparing them to be ethical leaders.

Community Service

Through programs like our Pay It Forward campaign, we instill a sense of social responsibility and compassion.

Innovation

We embrace modern teaching methods and technology, including robotics and digital learning tools.
