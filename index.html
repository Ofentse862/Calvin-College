Welcome to Calvin College

Located in the heart of Burgersfort, Limpopo, Calvin College stands as a beacon of educational excellence, dedicated to nurturing young minds from Grade R through Grade 12. Since our founding in 1998, we have remained steadfast in our commitment to providing quality independent education that transforms lives and builds futures.

Our approach to education goes beyond textbooks and examinations. We are dedicated to maximizing each learner's unique abilities within a carefully structured framework that emphasizes cooperation, mutual respect, and academic rigour. At Calvin College, we understand that every learner is different, with their own strengths, challenges, and aspirations.

We believe in empowering our learners with the confidence and skills necessary to navigate an ever-changing world. Through our comprehensive curriculum, experienced educators, and supportive learning environment, we help young people develop into resourceful, capable individuals who are prepared to manage their own lives and contribute meaningfully to society. Our learners graduate not just with qualifications, but with the critical thinking skills, work ethic, and self-assurance needed to thrive in higher education and the competitive world of employment.

Guided by our motto Naviter, Simpliciter, Parce - diligently, simply, moderately - we strive to instill values that serve our learners throughout their lives, helping them achieve excellence without losing sight of balance and integrity.
