Academic Excellence at Calvin College

At Calvin College, we provide a comprehensive, rigorous academic programme aligned with the National Curriculum and Assessment Policy Statement (CAPS). Our educational offering spans from Grade R through Grade 12, ensuring continuity and excellence at every stage of your child's learning journey.

We believe that quality education goes beyond simply covering the syllabus. Our experienced educators employ dynamic teaching methodologies that engage learners, foster critical thinking, and develop problem-solving skills essential for success in the 21st century. Through a carefully structured curriculum, modern facilities, and a commitment to individual learner development, we prepare our young people not just for examinations, but for life beyond the classroom - equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to excel in tertiary education and their chosen careers.

Foundation Phase

Grade R - Grade 3: Building strong literacy and numeracy foundations for young learners.

Intermediate Phase

Grade 4 - Grade 6: Developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills across subjects.

Senior Phase

Grade 7 - Grade 9: Preparing learners for FET with comprehensive subject offerings.

FET Phase

Grade 10 - Grade 12: Specialization and preparation for tertiary education and careers.

CAPS Curriculum

Calvin College follows the National Curriculum and Assessment Policy Statement (CAPS), ensuring that our learners receive a comprehensive, nationally recognized education that prepares them for success in higher education and beyond.
