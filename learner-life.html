Beyond the Classroom

At Calvin College, we believe that true education extends far beyond academic achievement. Our comprehensive extracurricular programme is designed to develop well-rounded individuals who are confident, capable, and ready to contribute to society in meaningful ways.

Through diverse opportunities in sports, arts, culture, leadership, and community engagement, our learners discover and develop their unique talents, build lasting friendships, learn valuable life skills such as teamwork and perseverance, and create memories that will stay with them long after they leave our school. Every learner is encouraged to participate in extracurricular activities, as we have seen time and again how these experiences contribute to personal growth, self-confidence, and academic success. Whether your child's passion lies in athletics, creative arts, academic competitions, or community service, Calvin College provides the platform, resources, and guidance necessary for them to excel and find their place in our vibrant school community.
